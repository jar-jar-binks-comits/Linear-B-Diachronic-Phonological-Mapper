About Linear B Diachronic Mapper

What is Linear B?

Linear B is a syllabic script used by the Mycenaean Greeks between approximately 1450-1200 BCE. It was primarily used for administrative records on clay tablets found at sites including Knossos, Pylos, Mycenae, and Thebes.
